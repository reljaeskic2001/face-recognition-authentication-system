
USER IMAGE SETUP INSTRUCTIONS

To use the face authentication system with your own identity, place approximately 15 images of your face inside this folder.

Recommended image requirements:

* Use clear, high-quality images.
* Only one face should be visible in each image.
* Include different head positions:

  * Looking straight at the camera
  * Slightly left
  * Slightly right
  * Looking up
  * Looking down
* Include different facial expressions:

  * Neutral
  * Smiling
  * Serious
* Include variations in lighting conditions.
* If you wear glasses, include both glasses and non-glasses images when possible.
* Ensure your face occupies a significant portion of the image.

Example:

user/
├── 00.jpg
├── 01.jpg
├── 02.jpg
├── ...
└── 14.jpg

After adding your images:

1. Run `embeddings.py` to regenerate the facial embedding database.
2. Run `authenticate.py` to launch the authentication system.
3. The system will then be able to recognize and authenticate your identity.

For best results, use at least 10–15 images captured from different angles and under different lighting conditions.
